### Research Topic: PIM-based AI Accelerator and Software Stack for Efficient LLM Inference

- **Internship Focused on Early-Stage Research & Development**  
  - Conducted in-depth research on Processing-In-Memory (PIM) AI accelerator architectures aimed at optimizing inference for **Large Language Models (LLMs)**.
  - Analyzed and reviewed state-of-the-art approaches to address key performance bottlenecks in LLM inference.
  - Implemented experimental setups to replicate evaluation results, gaining practical experience with the hardware and software components of PIM-based systems.
  - Developed novel research proposals to enhance PIM system performance in multi-device environments, with an intense focus on scalability, parallelism, and efficiency.
